Our Mission

At Galaxo Games we are very passionate about developing games. Therefore, we would like to become a game company which is able to entertain our customers by putting a smile on their faces, whilst learning new ways and advancing in this era.

From the start of semester 7 Game Development in the year of 2022, 6 Software engineers rose to become Galaxo Games. They had no idea the future will hold for them, but they know they can tackle it with a laugh.
